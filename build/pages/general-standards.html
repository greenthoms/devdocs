General Standards

Browser Support

SynWeb v67 is optimised for IE10+, Chrome, Safari and Firefox.

Screen Resolution

  • The minimum screen resolution for SynWeb v67 is 1024x768.
  • Layouts should consider the other common screen resolutions. Including: 1366x768 and 1920x1080

Device support

SynWeb v67 aims to support tablets and laptops with touch input.
