Description:

School Camp in the Grampian Ranges. Staying at bush campsites from Rocklands to the Black Range in Grampians National Park.

Attire & Packing List:

Attire will depend on the activities and includes the following:
- Warm jumpers and trousers
- Hiking books or shoes suitable for hiking
- Thick socks for hiking
- Swimsuit and towel
School uniform is not required for this excursion.
